# LATTICE Review Roles

Public changes should be reviewed through four lenses:

- **Semantic Algebra Reviewer** - closure, meet, join, order, and identity laws.
- **Evidence Custody Reviewer** - provenance, rights, receipts, and source boundaries.
- **Budget Safety Reviewer** - explicit frontiers and failures instead of silent loss.
- **API Stability Reviewer** - product-neutral contracts and compatibility.
